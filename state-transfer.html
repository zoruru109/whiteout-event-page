State Transfer Event

Whiteout Survival Sneak Peek — Transfer your City to a new State

Event Overview

The State Transfer event allows Chiefs to relocate their City to another State within their transfer group and begin a new journey with different alliances and communities.

Start: Mar 15 2026 End: Mar 21 2026 Duration: 7 Days Timezone: UTC
Group Power Caps depend on server progress and will be confirmed when the event begins.

Transfer Phases

Phase I — Pre-Transfer

Duration: 3 Days

  • Transfer Managers set a Power Cap threshold for entry.
  • Chiefs exceeding the cap can only transfer by invitation.
  • Players may preview destination states and availability.
  • States are categorized as Leading or Ordinary.

Phase II — Invitational Transfer

Duration: 2 Days

  • Transfer Managers may send invitations.
  • Two types: Ordinary Invite and Special Invite.
  • Allows early transfer before the general opening.

Phase III — Transfer Opens

Duration: 2 Days

  • All eligible Chiefs may transfer freely.
  • Must meet the destination State Power Cap.
  • No invitation required.

Transfer Requirements

  • Transfers require Transfer Passes.
  • The number needed depends on your Transfer Score.
  • Higher power relative to the target state requires more passes.
  • Transfer Passes can be obtained through packs or the Alliance Shop.

Transfer Score

A State’s Transfer Score equals the combined Transfer Score of its top 100 active Chiefs. States with the highest scores are classified as Leading States, while others are categorized as Ordinary States.

Important Notes

  • Transfers can only occur between states within the same transfer group.
  • Some game events unlock only after server progress milestones.
  • These features may not activate simultaneously across all servers.
  • Always confirm conditions once the event begins.
