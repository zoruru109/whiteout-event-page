Event modules

A clean, page-friendly summary of the featured activities. (Images are pulled from the source page.)

Feast Overture

Get ingredients and cook dishes with allies to kick off the celebration.

  • Earn ranking rewards and limited prizes by contributing dishes.
  • Tip: Use Alliance Fealty and Daily Specials for more points.
  • Event item: Feast Bag (random ingredient; converts to a 10K Resource Supply Chest after the event).

Gourmet Spread

Submit dishes to gain alliance points and unlock rewards; time-limited packs offer extra rewards.

  • Participate and submit dishes to obtain alliance points and unlock rewards.
  • Limited packs available during the event: Celebration Delicacies & Festive Delights.
  • Unclaimed rewards are mailed after the event or upon leaving the alliance.

Chiefs of the Dawn

A 7-stage competition across key progression activities.

  • Stages: Power Boost, Beast Slay, Hero Development, Troop Training, Hero Gear, Gather Resources, Chief Gear.
  • Each stage lasts 1 day; earn points and claim milestone rewards.
  • Stage ranking rewards each day; total ranking rewards at the end.

Celebratory Symphony

Complete missions and lines to earn chests; finish everything for the Banquet Reliquary.

  • Complete designated missions to earn rewards.
  • Complete a full vertical or horizontal line for a stage chest.
  • Complete all missions to obtain the Banquet Reliquary.

Top-up Benefits

Accumulate Top Up Points to earn special rewards during the celebration.

  • Track your top-up milestones and claim rewards as you progress.

Trail of Taste

Find ingredients through common gameplay actions (beasts, gathering, gems, speedups).

  • Possible sources include spending gems, using speedups, gathering resources, and defeating beasts.

Celebration Firework

Set off fireworks in your city or another Chief’s city.

  • Using multiple fireworks at once doesn’t extend duration—each triggers a new firework.

Snapshot of Time

A celebratory feature highlighted during the anniversary period.

  • Collect the themed rewards and visuals while the event window lasts.

Dawn Gala

Top alliances can host a one-hour feast after the event.

  • Top 10 alliances on the Feast Overture leaderboard may host an Alliance Feast.
  • Venues vary by ranking: Feastmaster’s Hall (Top 1), Celebration Square (2–3), Banquet Pavilion (4–10).
  • Members can only participate in their own alliance’s feast during the tally phase.

Kasia’s Wish House

Earn vouchers from commissions or packs, then spend them for rewards.

  • Regular wishes build Wish Points; spend them for a “Sincere Wish” that lands on a Mythic corner prize.
  • Redeem Dream Marks in Kasia’s Shop for big rewards.
  • Unused Wish Points: every 100 converts to one Sincere Wish at event end; below 100 resets.
